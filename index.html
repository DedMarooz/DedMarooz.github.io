05 — Method

How I Work With Data

A dashboard is never the goal. A decision is.

01

Start with the business question

What decision does this data need to support? I ask before I write a single query.

02

Define the metric clearly

A vague KPI gives a misleading answer. I make sure everyone agrees on what we are measuring.

03

Check the data first

I validate the source, the logic, and the edge cases before building anything on top of it.

04

Build it clean

Readable SQL and Python, and outputs that both engineers and managers understand.

05

End with a recommendation

I always say what I think the data means and what I would do next — not just show a chart.

06

Automate what repeats

If a question comes back every week, I turn it into a dashboard, a report, or an alert.
