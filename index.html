About Me

I spent 4 years at eToro building tools that trading teams rely on every day — dashboards that profile customers in real time, premarket briefings that help traders see the full picture before the bell, and automated pipelines that publish commodity commission data on schedule.

I know the stock market from the inside. I understand how positions are managed, how risk is hedged, and what questions traders are actually asking when they look at data. That domain knowledge makes my analysis actionable, not just accurate.

I am now expanding into AI — building assistants with the Claude API and training models for ad hoc analysis. I believe every analyst who wants to stay relevant needs to know how to work with AI tools, not just traditional BI.
