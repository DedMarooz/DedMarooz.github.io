About Me

I spent nearly 4 years at eToro working at the intersection of trading operations and data. I started as a Crypto System Operator — monitoring high-stakes trading systems around the clock and automating financial reporting with Python. I then grew into a Dealing Data Analyst role, building dashboards used daily by the trading room and training an internal Azure Databricks AI on company data to answer business questions in real time.

I have real trading domain knowledge — I understand how positions work, how risk is measured, and what questions actually matter to a dealing team. That background lets me build tools traders use, not just technically correct dashboards that nobody opens.

I hold a B.A. in Economics and Management (GPA 87.5, IMPACT! Scholarship) and built a Bitcoin price predictor using Twitter sentiment data for my final project. I use AI tools every day: to write code faster, automate repetitive tasks, and build smarter analytical workflows.
